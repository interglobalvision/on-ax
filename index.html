ON | AX

In monster metropolis Mexico City few producers achieve a distinct and fresh sound like A-rp has with his debut EP on MALIGNA. With "ON | AX" and its dense basslines, warped vocals and dark synths, A-rp creates dance music for the not too distant post-apocalyptic future. Dystopian club music is now.

Available on limited edition cassette and digitally on Bandcamp, iTunes, Google Play, Amazon and the best music streaming services.
